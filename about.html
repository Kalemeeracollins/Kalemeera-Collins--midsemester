About Jordern Junior Primary School

Where tradition meets innovation in education.

Our Story

Jordern Junior Primary School has been a beacon of educational excellence since its inception in 1990. With a mission to inspire and nurture young minds, we provide a holistic approach to education, combining academic rigor with extracurricular activities.

Our dedicated staff and vibrant community ensure that every child thrives and achieves their full potential in a safe and nurturing environment.
